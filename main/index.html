Latest News

December 7, 2023

WobbleWorks Inc, the creator of 3Doodler, the original 3D printing pen, today announced that Mark Ivory joins the company as Chief Commercial Officer.

As WobbleWorks new CCO, Mark Ivory will lead and scale the company’s growth and global business operations and go-to-market teams, including all sales, marketing, and customer service. He joins the company’s C-suite, teaming up with co-founders Daniel Cowen, CEO and Maxwell Bogue, President & CTO.

Mark brings a wealth of experience to WobbleWorks, having held senior leadership positions across a range of consumer product industries, including kitchenware, optical, fine writing instruments and personal accessories. He has a proven track record of success in driving operational excellence and delivering strong financial results.

“We are thrilled to welcome Mark to the WobbleWorks team,” said Daniel Cowen, CEO at Wobbleworks. “Mark brings vast consumer product experience scaling global business growth and driving operational excellence.” Before joining 3Doodler, Mark was the Executive Chairman at Meyer Group, consulted for the board of FGX International/Essilor, and was Chief Commercial Officer with AT Cross Company..

“Wobbleworks is an innovative market-leader which, under the 3Doodler brand, designs best-in-class products offering limitless creative delight for all ages, combined with an educational and fun experience,” said Mark Ivory, CCO at 3Doodler. “I am delighted to join the team and look forward to building on the company’s success and brand equity.”
